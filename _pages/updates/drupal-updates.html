Update modules, and drupal core

Drupal 9 requires at least PHP 7.3. PHP 7.4 is also supported but not required.

If you are running Drupal 9 on Apache, at least version 2.4.7 is required.

You can read detail Environment requirements of Drupal 9 https://www.drupal.org/docs/understanding-drupal/how-drupal-9-is-made-and-what-is-included/environment-requirements-of

Step I: Backup website & Enable maintenance mode

Back up both your files and database. Having a complete backup makes it easy to revert to the prior version if the update fails.

Enable maintenance mode:

- Using Drupal, put your site into maintenance mode. To do so:

- Navigate the admin interface to Administration > Configuration > Development > Maintenance mode.

- Check "Put site into maintenance mode" checkbox.

- Click on "Save configuration".
